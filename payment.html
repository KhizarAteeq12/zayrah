Payment Options

Cash on Delivery (COD)

Pay When You Receive Your Order

We understand the importance of trust and convenience in online shopping. That's why we offer Cash on Delivery (COD) service all over Pakistan.

With COD, you don't need to make any advance payments. Simply place your order online, and pay the full amount in cash when your package is delivered right to your doorstep.

How COD Works:

  1. Browse our store and add desired products to your cart.
  2. Proceed to checkout and select "Cash on Delivery" as your payment method.
  3. Provide your shipping details.
  4. Confirm your order.
  5. We will process and dispatch your order.
  6. Our delivery partner will bring the package to your address.
  7. You inspect the package (ensure it's sealed) and pay the exact order amount to the delivery person.

Is COD Available in My City?

Yes, our Cash on Delivery service is available across all major cities, towns, and deliverable areas throughout Pakistan. If you can receive courier service, you can use COD!
